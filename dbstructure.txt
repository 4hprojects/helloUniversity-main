MongoDB (tblUsers):
├── _id (user unique ID)
├── email
├── password (hashed)
├── isVerified
├── verificationToken
└── createdAt

Supabase (users_profile):
├── id (same as MongoDB _id)
├── firstName
├── lastName
├── phone
├── profilePicture
└── updatedAt

Supabase (courses):
├── courseId
├── userId (FK to users_profile)
├── courseTitle
└── ...

Supabase (enrollments):
├── enrollmentId
├── userId (FK)
├── courseId (FK)
└── ...
